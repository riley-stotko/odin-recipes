Waffle Recipe

Description

Waffles are another timeless classic, even though they require a few more ingredients and take more time to make does not mean they are any less delicious then some of the easier breakfast options. This long time favorite is perfect for Sunday breakfast and can be frozen to be eaten throughout the week, simply toast them.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups buttermilk
  • 1/3 cup butter (melted)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat waffle iron
  2. Mix dry ingredients together in a large mixing bowl
  3. In a separate bowl crack eggs and mix wet ingredients
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients in the large mixing bowl
  5. Pour batter onto waffle iron

To make add blueberries or any other additions add them by pouring a layer of batter onto the iron then adding the fruit then pour another layer over the fruit before closing the iron.
