French Toast Recipe

Description

This sweet and tasty dish is known for turing old bread into a delicious breakfast. While you don't always have to use old bread it creates a better french toast
because it is drier and therefore absorbs the egg mixture better then fresh bread. French toast is a fast and high quality dish, only requiring a few common
household ingredients not much prep or cook time it is perfect for family breakfasts. Below is the best recipe anyone can find.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • cinnamon (as much as you would like)
  • 6-10 slices of bread
  • butter for the pan

Steps

  1. Get a large bowl or square container
  2. While mixing your ingredients turn your pan on at medium heat
  3. Pour milk into the container
  4. crack eggs in separate bowl, beat them well then add them to the milk
  5. Add vanilla to eggs and milk
  6. Add cinnamon

After mixing, butter your pan and dunk bread slices in the mixture until both sides are thoroughly coated.
Cook on pan until both sides are golden brown and sizzling on the edges. Serve with syrup and butter
