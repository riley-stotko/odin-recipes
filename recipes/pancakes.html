Pancake Recipe

Description

Pancakes are a timeless classic, one of the original traditional breakfasts. Known for being fluffy and light, pancakes are an all around favorite while being a classic on their own
things can be added to them to make it even better from chocolate to fruit there are very few ways for you to not make a good pancake. Listed below is one of the best recipes around.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg (beaten)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Butter for the pan

Steps

  1. Heat a pan at medium heat
  2. Stir all your dry ingredients together in a mixing bowl
  3. Crack and beat egg in a separate bowl add oil and milk
  4. Pour egg, milk, and oil into dry ingredients bowl
  5. Stir it all together

Cook until both sides are golden brown, serve with syrup and butter or the topping of your choice.
Add blueberries while pancakes are cooking to make blueberry pancakes.
